# The Wellness Studio

The Wellness Studio is a collection of self-guided practices you can use any
time: breathing exercises, short meditations, gentle movement prompts, and
reflective journaling cues. Think of it as a calm corner of the app where you
choose what fits your moment.

## How to use it well

- Pick one practice rather than many. A single conscious breath counts.
- Notice what helps without turning it into a performance or a streak to
  protect.
- Pair a practice with a small intention, like "I want to feel steadier before
  my meeting."

## Building a rhythm

Many people find a loose routine helpful: a morning breath, a midday reset, and
an evening wind-down. The Studio adapts to your energy — some days rest is the
practice. None of this is medical treatment; it is support for everyday
wellbeing. If you are in crisis, please contact a local emergency service or
crisis line.
