# Your Wellness Journey

A journey in Velness is a gentle, self-paced path of small practices that build
on one another. Rather than goals to "win," journeys are invitations to notice
and return. Examples include a Calm week, a Sleep reset, or a Kindness to self
theme.

## How a journey works

- Each day offers one small, doable step — a breath, a reflection, a pause.
- Progress is about showing up, not perfection. Missed days simply resume.
- Reflections you write become part of your memory, so future suggestions can
  feel personal without sharing anything outside your account.

## Making it sustainable

Tie a practice to an existing habit ("after my coffee, one box breath"). Keep
expectations kind. If a theme stops fitting, switch — the point is what helps
you, not completing a track. Journeys support wellbeing; they are not therapy or
a diagnostic tool.
