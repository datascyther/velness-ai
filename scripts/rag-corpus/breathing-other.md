# Other Breathing Exercises

Beyond box breathing, two gentle patterns are helpful for daily calm.

## 4-7-8 breathing

- Inhale through the nose for a count of 4.
- Hold for a count of 7.
- Exhale through the mouth for a count of 8.

The longer exhale activates the body's relaxation response. Use it once or
twice when having trouble settling, not as a rapid sequence.

## Coherent (resonance) breathing

Breathe at about 5-6 breaths per minute — roughly a 5-second inhale and 5-second
exhale, with no forced hold. This steady rhythm is associated with a calmer
heart-rate variability and is easy to sustain for several minutes.

## Gentle guidance

Find a comfortable seat, soften your shoulders, and let the breath be smooth
rather than forced. Stop if you feel lightheaded. These are wellness practices
and not a replacement for medical advice.
