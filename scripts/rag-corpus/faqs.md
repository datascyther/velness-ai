# Wellness FAQ

**Is this a substitute for therapy?**
No. Velness offers self-guided wellness and psychoeducation. It does not
diagnose or treat conditions. For clinical care, please consult a qualified
professional.

**What do I do in a crisis?**
If you are thinking about harming yourself or others, contact your local
emergency number or a crisis line right away. You deserve immediate support.

**Are my reflections private?**
Your journaling and reflections stay tied to your account and are used only to
personalize your experience within the app. They are not sold or used to make
medical decisions.

**How often should I practice?**
A little often is better than a lot rarely. Even one minute of breathing or a
single reflection counts.

**Why does the app suggest different things?**
Suggestions adapt to your stated goals, recent reflections, and the practices
you engage with — all within your account, with no external diagnosis.
